## I got it vs (You) got it

{% image "/images/english/speaking/You-got-it.jpg", width="700px", alt="a toy you liked in your childhood" %}

> I get it - 我明白了之前不明白的事情
> I got it - 我来吧 (我有把握做某事)
> (You ) Got it - （好的收到，你放心）
